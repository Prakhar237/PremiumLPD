— Appendix · Questions

Asked,
often.

Do I need any technical skill to launch a site?

Not at all. If you can copy-paste a line of text at your domain registrar, you can launch a site here. Hosting, templates, SEO and content are all handled for you.

Can I change templates after launch?

Yes. Switch between Domain Blogger, Business Blog, and Product Autoblog at any time. All your content carries over — no rebuilding.

Who actually writes the articles?

Our autoblog engine. It researches keywords for your niche, drafts the article, and publishes on your approved schedule. You can review each piece, edit, or enable full autopilot.

Will Google penalise automated content?

Only when it's thin or spammy. Our content is researched, structured with schema, internally linked, and passes helpful-content heuristics. Current customers rank on page one for hundreds of terms.

I have fifty-plus domains. Is that supported?

Very much so. The Portfolio and Agency plans are designed for this — one dashboard, unlimited domains, each with its own niche, branding and content pipeline.

What if I want to leave later?

Full export, one click. Take your content as WordPress XML, Ghost JSON, or plain HTML. The domain, content and revenue are always yours.
