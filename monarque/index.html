— Appendix

Quietly asked.

How is this different from guest posting or a PR agency?+

Guest posts are one-off, mostly on low-authority sites, and disappear from search attention within weeks. PR agencies buy reach in publications, not rankings. MoneyTrainDomains is a permanent, coordinated placement across a network we own — SEO-engineered blogs and contextual backlinks built specifically to capture commercial-intent search queries and convert them.

Is this white-hat? Is there any risk to my brand?+

Every domain in our network is aged, editorially active, and independently authoritative. Content is long-form, original, and on-topic — indistinguishable from standard editorial. No link schemes, no exact-match spam, no manipulation that would trigger a penalty.

How long before I see movement in rankings?+

First placements are live within 14 days. Page-one movement on commercial terms typically begins at day 30–45. Our average client reaches peak organic lift at 90–120 days, and placements compound indefinitely afterwards.

Do I own the content? Can I republish?+

The content lives on our network domains — that's what gives it authority. You receive a licensed, exportable copy of every placement for your own records and press kits, and we share monthly performance data via a private dashboard.

Do you work with competitors in the same vertical?+

One category-exclusive engagement per vertical, per quarter. When you engage, that category is locked to you across the network for the duration of the engagement.

What does an engagement actually cost?+

Foundation is $5,000/month (3-month minimum). Authority is $10,000/month (6-month minimum). Marquee Domain Promotion is custom-priced — upfront fee plus success commission, scoped to the asset. Get in touch and we'll prepare a tailored proposal after a first call.
